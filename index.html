Action Almala3b

FOOTBALL CARD GAME

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Build a complete formation of 11 players and win! 🏆

🏆 WIN CONDITIONS — ALL 3 REQUIRED

CONDITION 01
Valid Formation
4-3-3 4-4-2 5-3-2
CONDITION 02
At least one Captain ⭐C
CONDITION 03
A star in each line
A star ⭐ in Defense, Midfield & Attack — or consecutive numbers in any line:
Defense: 2-3-4 or 3-4-5  |  Midfield: 6-7-8  |  Attack: 9-10-11

🎮 HOW TO PLAY

  • 1Each player starts with 5 cards
  • 2Each turn: draw 2 cards, then play up to 2 cards — or Skip
  • 3Hand limit: max 7 cards
  • 4Field limit: max 11 players

🗑️
